How it works

Cloud-native, without the learning curve

Get the ideal cloud-native set up with Kubernetes and AWS without spending months figuring out all the tools and configurations you need to get running in production

Once you have the infrastructure, you now need to keep it patched and updated. The EKS control plane, nodes, and all the tools like Argo CD need to be regularly upgraded. NotOps automates all of this at a regular cadence.

Battle-tested cloud-native tooling

Our Story

Our team has been building software for decades and cloud-native environments since the early years of orchestration tools. We’ve seen companies run out of money, lose customers and lose their entire teams because of badly designed, unstable platforms. Spending millions of dollars and months or years getting nowhere. We came to realize that as more and more companies move to the cloud, there is a need for “one-click” solutions that don’t require learning all of the cloud-native tools and AWS. Most of the companies need a similar set of tools, or at least have a least common denominator.


NotOps was born out of this belief that it shouldn’t take months and years of struggling to get a production grade platform running, and there shouldn’t be an ongoing high-cost to keeping the platform patched and upgraded.
